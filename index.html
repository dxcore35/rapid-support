Rapid: Speed Reading

Read Faster, Stay Focused

Rapid is a focused speed-reading app built on RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation), a method used in cognitive science and vision research for decades.

Instead of scanning full lines, you keep your eyes in one place while words appear at a fixed focal point, reducing unnecessary eye movement and visual friction.

Science-backed approach

RSVP is a well-studied experimental method. Peer-reviewed literature indicates that reducing saccadic eye movement can improve reading rate in many conditions.

Comprehension depends on pacing and text difficulty, so Rapid includes timing controls such as punctuation pauses to help you balance speed and understanding.

Core features

  • One-word reader with center-letter emphasis and punctuation pause controls
  • Quick Add from PDF, URL, text, or clipboard
  • Library with resume progress and remaining-time estimate
  • Reading stats: words read, speed trends, sessions, and time saved

Additional features

  • Advanced haptic feedback system
  • Share extension for reading text and web links from anywhere
  • Home Screen widgets for instant rapid reading
  • Siri Shortcuts for advanced automations
